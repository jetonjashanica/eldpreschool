About Us

Learn About Us

Best Prechool For Your Kids

A warm welcome to ELD Preschool, where we are passionate about learning and celebrating individual achivements. At our ELD Preschool we are passionate about developing the "whole child". Teaching life skills that will enable children to thrive, not only in our school, but throughout their education and into adult life. We are dedicated to providing high quality education which will enable all children to enjoy learning and to achieve their personal best.

At our ELD Preschool, we are dedicated to providing high-quality education enabling all children to enjoy learning and achieve their personal best.


Key development

  • Communication and language
  • Physical development
  • Personal, social and emotional development
  • Literacy
  • Mathematics
  • Understanding the world
  • Expressive arts and design

We do this through

  • Ensuring every child feels valued
  • Prioritising the well-being of children
  • Providing high quality learning experiences within a creative and engaging curriculum
  • Ensuring a safe and fully inclusive environment that encourages all children to shine
  • Building resilient and independent learners that are confident to tackle new challenges
  • Developing creativity and collaboration by encouraging children to communicate and question their learning, and the world around them
  • Setting high expectations for behavior and learning, whilst recognizing individual achievements and progress
  • Showing respect for each other within a caring and nurturing environment
  • Working together in partnership with parents and carers to enable each child to succeed
  • Ensuring children are equipped with key skills, both academic and personal, to engage and embrace future responsibilities and experiences
